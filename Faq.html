Information about the release of Slurp.

Slurp is the BEST way to experience Chapter 1 and Chapter 2 Fortnite seasons!

There is NO specific launch date, but expect public testing in the near future. Official Launch shouldnt be longer than a few weeks max.

Slurp will host multiple seasons at the same time. As of the offical launch, it will ONLY be season 6, until the playerbase grows large enough for us to launch Season 14 along with it. We do not expect that wait to be very long!

All developers work are checked before implementation or release to public. Milxnor makes sure files are safe before releasing them for our community to use!

You will earn V-Bucks by getting kills, wins, and completing special challenges! You will also be able to purchase the battlepass using the V-Bucks you earn in-game!

We plan to run 24-7 Servers with instant-queues on every game mode, Solos, Duos, Squads, LTM (FOR EACH SEASON).
